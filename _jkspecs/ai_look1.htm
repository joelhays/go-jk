LookForOppositingTarget

MotS

Number of arguments: 2

Example:

LookForOpposingTarget	500.0, 0.0

Looks for a target with a contrary Alignment variable. Used e.g. by Rebel soldiers in MotS to attack Stormtroopers.

Arguments:

Argument # Argument meaning
1 Interval for look (msec)
2 Time to sleep before check again (msec)
