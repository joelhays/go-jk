Leap

(MotS)

Number of arguments: 6

Example:

Leap			1500.0,	0.7, 1.00, 0.40, 3000.0, 3.5

Makes an attacking leap. Used e.g. by Vornskr.

Arguments:

Argument # Argument meaning
1 Interval between leaps
2 Min Dot for leap. 1.0=must be facing exactly, 0.0=180 degrees arc, -1.0=full 360 degrees arc.
3 Max distance from target
4 Min distance from target
5 Interval between waking up and first leap
6 Leap speed (JKU/sec)
